SEEKING ADVENTURES

Antarctic Expedition

Explore the frozen wilderness of Antarctica. Witness breathtaking landscapes and unique wildlife.



Activities: Kayaking, camping, wildlife photography, research visits.

Duration: 10-14 days


Amazon Survival Trek

Survive in the heart of the Amazon Rainforest. Learn survival skills, trek through jungles, and discover biodiversity.


Activities: Jungle trekking, shelter building, wildlife tracking.

Duration: 7-10 days


Mongolian Nomadic Expedition

Experience nomadic life in Mongolia's vast steppes. Live with local nomads, ride horses, and explore the Gobi Desert.


Activities: Horseback riding, camel trekking, herding, stargazing.

Duration: 14-21 days
